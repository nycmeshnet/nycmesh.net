---
title: "What is your business plan?"
---
We are not a business and we are not an ISP! We don’t have “customers” or “users”, we have members. Being a member of our network has many benefits including replacing your current ISP with real, unmonitored internet access. Businesses are free to join or connect to the mesh as long as they obey the [network commons license.](https://nycmesh.net/ncl.pdf)
