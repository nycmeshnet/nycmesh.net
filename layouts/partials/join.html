Get Connected

Connecting to NYC Mesh will give you access to the Internet with speeds of up to 100Mbps. To get connected, you’ll need a line of sight from your rooftop or window to one of our supernodes or hubs, or to a LinkNYC kiosk. To see if you’re in a coverage zone, check out our map. If you’re not yet in range, keep checking back as our network is always expanding, or volunteer to help us grow towards your neighborhood. Once you get connected, all we ask is that you give a minimum monthly donation of $20.

There are three ways you can get connected:

Method 1: Request a Volunteer-Led Install

For a $160 one-time fee, our all-volunteer team can install hardware on your rooftop or outside your window to get you connected. Read the FAQ to learn about volunteer-led installs and then fill out the form below.

For questions on an existing request please email us and include your node number.


Once you submit this form, we will get back to you requesting a panorama photo from your rooftop or window to assess your line of sight to nearby hubs. If you don’t receive a response within five minutes, check your email’s spam folder and mark the email "not spam."

By submitting this request you agree to have your node published on the map.

Method 2: Organize a Building or Multiple-Unit Install

We’ve worked with co-ops, management companies, block associations and hotels to connect entire buildings and city blocks. To work with us to organize a large scale install, contact us on email or Slack

Method 3: Do It Yourself Install

Already know something about networking? Follow the instructions on our docs and ask for help on our Slack if you’d like to try connecting to the network on your own.
